MONTREAL CRAZY DRIVER

'MONTREAL CRAZY DRIVER' is a game where you control a your car to jump over cones using the spacebar Your goal is to collect points by jumping over as many cones as possible without crashing into it, and compete for high scores. This game is all about beating your own records and having a "fun" montreal driving experience !!
